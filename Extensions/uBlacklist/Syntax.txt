
1. Domains

Hides ccTLDs from search results: 
/.*\.(ab|ac|ad|ae|af)($|\/)/

Hides gTLDs from search results: 
/.*\.(baby|baidu|banamex|bananarepublic|barcelona|barclaycard|barclays|barefoot|bargains|baseball|basketball|bauhaus|bayern)($|\/)/

Hides specific domains from search results: 
/.*\.(bbc|bloomberg|businessnews)\.(com|net)($|\/)/i

Highlights specific domains or TLDs in search results:
@1*://*.github.com/*
@2*://*.gov/*
@2*://*.edu/*

2. Keywords

Hide domains from search results if a specific keyword is found in the URL
/(astro(logy|scope|theme)?|horoscope|tarot)/i

...
astro(logy|scope|theme)? = astrology, astroscope, astrotheme
