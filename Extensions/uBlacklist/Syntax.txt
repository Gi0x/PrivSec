
1. DOMAINS

> Hides ccTLDs from results: /.*\.(ab|ac|ad|ae|af)($|\/)/
> Hides gTLDs from results: /.*\.(baby|baidu|banamex|bananarepublic|barclaycard|bauhaus|bayern)($|\/)/
> Hides domains from results: /.*\.(bbc|bloomberg|businessnews)\.(com|net)($|\/)/i
> Highlights specific domains or TLDs in results:

@1*://*.github.com/*
@2*://*.gov/*
@2*://*.edu/*

2. KEYWORDS

> EX 1+2 - with ?

/(^|[-_/])(cunt(bag|s|whore|y)?)([-_/]|$)/i

Matches: cunt,cuntbag,cunts,cuntwhore,cunty

/(^|[-_/])(lube(ed|ing|s)?)([-_/]|$)/i

Matches: lube,lubed,lubing,lubes
Use this syntax ONLY if the root word is unambiguous

> EX 3 - without ?

/(^|[-_/])(ass(es|face|grab|hole))([-_/]|$)/i

Precisely matches: asses,assface,assgrab,asshole but NOT ass itself
Omit ? here because the root word is ambiguous and can form part of other words, ex. assign




/(^|[-_/])(celeb(s|rity|rities)|gossip)([-_/]|$)/i ...use this, hides more domains
